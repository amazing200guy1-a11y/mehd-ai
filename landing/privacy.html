Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

At Mehd AI ("we", "us", "our"), we are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our platform.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Email address, name, and authentication credentials when you register.
  • Trading Data: Currency pairs analyzed, trade decisions, and consensus results generated within the platform.
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app, features used, and session information.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and browser type for compatibility and security purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the Mehd AI platform and its services.
  • To improve the AI consensus engine and risk management algorithms.
  • To process payments and manage subscriptions.
  • To detect and prevent fraud, unauthorized access, and security threats.
  • To communicate important updates about the platform.

3. Data Protection

All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256). We implement Firebase Authentication for secure sign-in, and all API communications use HMAC-SHA256 signed intent capsules. Your broker credentials are never stored on our servers.

4. What We Never Do

  • We never sell your personal data to third parties.
  • We never share your trading data with other users.
  • We never store your broker passwords or API keys on our infrastructure.
  • We never access your brokerage account funds.

5. Third-Party Services

We use Firebase (Google) for authentication and hosting, and Paystack for payment processing. These services have their own privacy policies and are GDPR compliant.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all personal data is permanently removed within 30 days.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. Contact us at privacy@mehdai.com to exercise these rights.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes via the platform or email.

9. Contact

For privacy concerns, contact: privacy@mehdai.com
